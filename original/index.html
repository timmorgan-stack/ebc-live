At Empire Broadcasting Corporation, we offer state-of-the-art equipment, experienced sound engineers, and comfortable accommodations for large ensembles. Our studio features world class acoustics and is fully equipped for scoring to picture, comfortably seating 40 musicians.

Create Your Masterpiece at East London's Premier Recording Studio, Featuring World-Class Acoustics and State-of-the-Art Equipment

  • With spacious seating arrangements and top-of-the-line equipment, we ensure that every musician feels at ease and can deliver their best performance.

    • Digital Recording: AVID Pro Tools HDX, Mac Pro (2019), Mac Mini (2020)

    • Console: LOOPTROTTER 40 Input In-Line Console

    • Pre Amps: Neve 88R, Neve 1073, API 512V/312, DAV BG501

    • EQ/Dynamics: Rupert Neve Designs 551, SSL 611EQ, Elysia XFilter

    • Microphones: Neumann U87 Ai, Telefunken ELA M260/TF51/M60 FET, Peluso P67, Schoeps Mk21

    • Monitoring: ATC SCM45a

    • Acoustic design by Roger D’arcy

    • Full gear list Equipment page

  • Our experienced sound engineers have collaborated with top artists like Arctic Monkeys, Taylor Swift, and Michael Bublé. They've also worked on major TV/film projects including Dr Who, BBC, and Netflix productions.

  • Nestled in East London, Empire Studio is perfect for superior music recording and performance. Our spacious studio accommodates orchestras and large ensembles, seating up to 40 musicians comfortably. Bathed in natural light, our live room and control room inspire creativity. Fully equipped for scoring to picture without compromising quality. Additional spaces include a dedicated vocal booth and an amp/soloist booth, making it ideal for any recording needs from full orchestras to solo performances.

Services

At Empire Broadcasting, we specialize in providing exceptional music services tailored to your needs. Whether you require music for film, orchestral sessions, choir sessions, bands, piano sessions, vocals, podcasts, or live and pre-recorded video sessions, our state-of-the-art recording studio and experienced team will bring your vision to life.

Music to Picture

We offer expert music composition and scoring services for films, TV shows, and commercials, ensuring that the music perfectly complements the visuals and enhances the overall storytelling.

Remote, Live & Pre-recorded Video Sessions

Utilising Listento software for streaming 32-bit PCM multichannel audio in real time, direct from any DAW to the world you can collaborate globally.

Orchestral

Our studio is fully equipped to accommodate full orchestras and larger ensembles, providing a spacious and acoustically optimized environment for recording and capturing the rich sound of live instruments.

Piano Sessions

Experience the iconic sound and dynamics of Steinway Model B Grand Piano. Also available for practise sessions.

Choir

Accommodating up to 40 vocalists, our spacious live room ensures unparalleled sound quality. With natural light and a supportive atmosphere, your choir can achieve its best performance. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated vocal booth provide the perfect setting to capture every nuance of your ensemble's sound.

Podcasts

Recording your mixed-media podcast in a beautiful environment with natural lighting and full RGB LED stage lights for dynamic backdrops.Our theatre seating even allows you to invite a live audience.
